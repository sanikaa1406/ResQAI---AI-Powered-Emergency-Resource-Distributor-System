About ResQAI

ResQAI is an AI-powered emergency response and assistance platform designed to enhance public safety, minimize response time, and provide intelligent support during critical situations.

In times of crisis—such as the COVID-19 pandemic—healthcare systems across the world faced significant challenges, including delayed emergency responses, lack of real-time coordination, and limited accessibility to immediate help. ResQAI aims to bridge this gap by leveraging the power of Artificial Intelligence.

The platform is built to:

⚡ Detect and respond to emergencies faster

📍 Provide real-time location-based assistance

🤖 Offer AI-driven guidance during critical situations

🚨 Connect users with nearby emergency services instantly

By combining technology with real-world emergency needs, ResQAI strives to create a smarter, faster, and more reliable support system that can save lives when every second matters.
